Coco Cookies

Description

Tasty coco cookies made with delicious dutch processed coco powder to give it a subtle coco flavor.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Dutch Processed Coco powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Granulated Sugar

Steps

  1. Add wet ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add dry ingredients in a separate bowl
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  4. Bake in oven at 350 degrees farenheit for 15 minutes.
