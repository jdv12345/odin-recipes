Pesto Pasta

Description

Pasta covered in a tasty pesto sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta in salted water for six minutes
  2. Combine basil, garlic, cheese, and olive oil in a blender
  3. Pour pesto over your pasta
  4. Enjoy the pasta!
