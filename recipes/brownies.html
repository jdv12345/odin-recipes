Brownies

Description

Delicious chocolate brownie with fudge and chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Dutch Processed Coco powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Add wet ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add dry ingredients in a separate bowl
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  4. Bake in oven at 400 degrees fahrenheit for 15 minutes.
